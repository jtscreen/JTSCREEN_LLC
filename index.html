Joshua Screen

Artist | Performer | Creator

While bios are typically short and concise, I’ve always felt they can come across as impersonal or overly formal. Rather than aiming for the “perfect” bio, I prefer to speak to you directly—because, after all, that’s what this is all about: connection.

I’m based in New York City, but I’m always open to travel for the right opportunity.

I thrive on challenge. For me, challenges are opportunities to grow, and I’m always eager to learn alongside passionate collaborators.

Art is my passion—creating it, sharing it, and helping others bring their artistic visions to life. While I won’t list every skill in this bio (I’ve curated more detailed profiles on my subpages for specific fields), here’s a quick overview of the areas where I can help make your ideas a reality:

  • Performance: Acting, singing, dancing
  • Photography: Headshots, productions, portraits, events
  • Music: Production, songwriting, performing
  • Web Design: I built this site from the ground up
  • Video Editing: Reels, YouTube videos, short films, and more

I look forward to working with you and helping bring your creative vision to life.
